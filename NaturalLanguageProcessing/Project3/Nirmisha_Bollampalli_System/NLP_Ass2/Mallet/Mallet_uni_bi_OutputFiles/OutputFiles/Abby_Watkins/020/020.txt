However work study applications are still available for certain classes The new deadline is April Each year about percent of Penland s students receive financial assistance Penland offers financial assistance in four categories general work study scholarships special work study scholarships studio assistantships and full scholarships General work study scholarship students receive housing meals and reduced tuition all other categories receive housing meals and tuition Although work study selection is made after the lottery some spaces are reserved in each class for work study students Scholarships are intended for students who would have difficulty attending Penland without financial assistance You may not apply as a full paying student if you are applying for a scholarship If you are not selected you may reapply as a full paying student if space is available Applications for scholarships and studio assistantships including letters of reference must be received by February applicants will be notified by the end of March Work Study Scholarships Work study students are an integral part of life at Penland This program is supported by Penland Friends foundations the sale of work donated to the end of session auctions the Alvin Lehman Foundation Fund the Claire Lucas Fund and Penland s general operating budget The work study program is designed to make Penland s resources available to the widest variety of students Each summer work study scholarships are awarded Work study students accept the challenge of balancing studio time with work for the school Work study students work hours per week on a variety of tasks principally in food service and dishwashing They receive dormitory accommodations meals and discounted tuition Most positions involve physical labor but there are a few which can accommodate physical limitations If you have physical limitations please send a separate note explaining what kinds of work you are able to do This will not factor into selection but will help us with work assignments if you are selected In addition to their regular duties work study students are required to work from AM to PM on the day before and the day after their session You are responsible for making travel arrangements which will allow you to meet this requirement If you cannot meet this requirement please do not apply Your letter of application should be no longer than two pages and should address the following areas Financial need why attending Penland would be a financial hardship for you Seriousness of intent your background and experience in craft if any prior experience in craft is not a requirement The individual artistic goals that you hope to pursue in each of the classes you are applying for Work ethic work study students have an experience which is different yet equally valuable from that of regular students Do you feel you will benefit from balancing studio work with physical labor for the school You will also need to supply a resum eacute and two letters of reference which address your work ethic and your commitment to learning Please see note above regarding physical limitations Applications must be received no later than February and must include your letters of reference After this deadline applicants will be considered only in the event of cancellations Work study students will be notified by the end of March and once accepted will be asked to submit full payment For procedures see application form Special Work Study Scholarships SUMMER CLASSES ONLY These funds provide a few fully supported work study scholarships for students in particular media at various stages in their career from a particular locale or from certain populations These scholarships carry the same work requirements as general work study but the student pays no tuition Recipients will be required to make a deposit which will be returned at the end of the session when work commitment is fulfilled Please send a letter resum eacute and two letters of recommendation In addition to the information outlined above your letter should explain your qualifications for the specific scholarships Some scholarships require images of your work Please send no more than five prints or slides no CDs Slides must be in a sleeve all slides or prints must by identified with your name Send a SASE for the return of these materials If you are not accepted for special work study you will still be considered for general work study You may use the same application and letter to apply for as many of these scholarships as you qualify for Janet
